# Compliance and governance

Union.ai maintains industry-recognized certifications and aligns its security practices with established frameworks. The platform's architecture — with strict data residency, tenant isolation, and control plane / data plane separation — inherently supports compliance requirements across regulated industries.

This section covers:

* [Certifications overview](./certifications-overview): Summary of all certifications and their status.
* [SOC 2 Type II](./soc2): Trust service criteria, audit coverage, and verified controls.
* [HIPAA compliance](./hipaa): How Union.ai supports HIPAA requirements for Protected Health Information.
* [GDPR alignment](./gdpr): Data residency and the EU-region deployment model.
* [Standards compliance](./standards-compliance): ISO 27001 and CIS benchmark control mappings.
* [Shared responsibility model](./shared-responsibility-model): Responsibility allocation for Self-Managed and BYOC deployments.
* [Trust Center](./trust-center): The Union.ai Trust Center at trust.union.ai.
